About me

Hi! My name is Qiqi (pronounced as kiki). I am a product designer and lettering artist. A third year student at Minerva Schools majoring in Data Science and minoring in History, I bring together analytical approach and humanistic insights to my design process.  I'm also enthusiast of Digital humanities, Education Technology, and Urbanism. 

Previously I've done design work at:
- a Series-B funded startup in San Francisco, @Scoop; 
- a refugee education NGO in Berlin, @Kiron;
- a UX agency in Argentina, @Aerolab;
- a new business incubator in Tokyo, @Hitomedia.  
Most recently, I am based in Hyderabad, India with the Minerva Schools. Check out my resume.

Outside of class and work, I am usually found exploring the city or on my way to explore the city. I enjoy analyzing the signage and wayfinding designs of transit systems, or wandering in unfamiliar neighborhoods people watching. And the third scenario is probably me doing lettering dreaming that I would be Jessica Hische some day.

You can find my recent travel snippets and lettering work on my Instagram @iiimkicky.  Say Hi! I'm alaways up for meeting new people and nerding out on design or urbanism.
